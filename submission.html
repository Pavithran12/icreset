    • Criteria for the submission of Abstracts

    Mark them down

    Proposals should be:

    Around 250 words in length.
    Reflective of at least one of the tracks of the conference.
    Relevant to the target audience.
    Original and offering insight, must be sent along with declaration by the author in Microsoft Word format (given in website)
    To allow for 'blind' evaluation by the selection panel.

    Paper should be

    In IEEE format (Template given in website)
    Drafted for 4 pages (Additional pages will be charged @ INR 100/page)

    Presentation should be:

    20 minutes of duration plus 10 minutes for Q&A
    Please note that the working language of the conference is English.
